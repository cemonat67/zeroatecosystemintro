Frequently Asked Questions

What is Zero@Ecosystem? ‚ñº
Zero@Ecosystem is a comprehensive platform of industry-specific carbon intelligence solutions. We help organizations across textiles, agriculture, manufacturing, healthcare, and more to measure, manage, and minimize their carbon footprint through AI-driven orchestration and real-time monitoring.
How does Zero@Ecosystem help with CSRD compliance? ‚ñº
Our platform automatically collects and organizes emissions data according to ESRS standards. We generate audit-ready reports, track Scope 1/2/3 emissions, and provide decarbonization roadmap tools to meet EU CSRD requirements.
What is a Digital Product Passport (DPP)? ‚ñº
A DPP is a digital record containing comprehensive information about a product's materials, production processes, carbon footprint, and end-of-life instructions. Required by EU regulations, DPPs enable full supply chain traceability and support circular economy initiatives.
How long does implementation take? ‚ñº
